# 📎 Appendix: Glossary

## Technical Terms Explained Simply

**Machine Learning**
- Computer system that learns from examples
- Gets better over time automatically
- No human rule-writing needed

**Fraud Score**
- Number from 0-100 (like a grade)
- 0 = Definitely legitimate
- 100 = Definitely fraud
- We typically block anything above 75

**False Positive**
- When we block a legitimate transaction
- Current industry average: 30%
- Our rate: Only 5%

**API Integration**
- The "connection" between your bank and our system
- Like connecting two computers
- Sends transaction data → Gets back decision

**Uptime**
- How much of the time the system is working
- 99.9% = System down only 9 hours per YEAR
- Industry standard for critical systems

**SLA (Service Level Agreement)**
- Our promise to you
- Specifies performance guarantees
- Financial penalties if we don't meet it
